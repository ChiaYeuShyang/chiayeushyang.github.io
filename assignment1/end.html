Stellar Nurseries

Nebulae are the birthplace and nursery of stars. They pump Hydrogen required for the stars to keep burning. Orion Nebula is known for a lot of Star Forming activity. The nebulae are very huge in size (like, super large). Orion is believed to be home to more than 1000 young stars.
